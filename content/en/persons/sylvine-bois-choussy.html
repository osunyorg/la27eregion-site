Sylvine Bois-Choussy joined The 27th Region in 2017 to develop its international activities, including the partnership with the Bloomberg Foundation. Interested in how local actors experiment and drive change in a globalised world, she previously led several European projects at the intersection of artistic, social and urban issues. She studied at La Sorbonne, the London School of Economics and the Institute of Higher Studies in Land Use Planning in Europe.
