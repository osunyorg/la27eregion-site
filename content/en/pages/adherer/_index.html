What La 27e Région and other similar initiatives in France and around the world advocate for is the possibility of creating spaces in which citizens, public agents and elected representatives may work together to explore how public policies are designed and focus the debate on how they are made: to do what? with whom? for whom? using what tools? We do not want to form a consulting firm, an academic research laboratory, a public agency, or a social enterprise (...) but rather to invent a framework that makes this reflective work possible, along with the tools that go with it.
Chantiers ouvert au public, La 27e Région
La Documentation française éditions, 2015.

Become a member
Why become a member
Becoming a member allows you to actively participate in the strategic direction of La 27e Région and to take care of this shared resource. It is also a way to stay informed about the latest news, to take part in a community with other members, or to get together for discussions and, of course, fun times.

What are your commitments as a member?
The only commitment you make is to be part of the adventure : as far as possible, participate in the annual general assembly, keep up to date with our news, and don't hesitate to ring the bell or pass on information if it seems relevant. In short, a member is someone who is committed, interested in the La 27e Région project, and shares its values. We ask for an annual membership fee of €40. However, money should not be a barrier, so please do not hesitate to contact us if this amount is too high for you. Membership of La 27e Région entitles you to:
Regular updates on La 27e Région's activities and the opportunity to discuss them with the team
Participation in the governance of La 27e Région, including the right to vote at the association's General Assembly

Invitations to thematic discussions (webinars) with other members and the La 27e Région team

The opportunity to benefit from our monitoring, awareness-raising, guidance, and networking activities

Priority access to professional events (international meetings, workshops, Public Innovation Month, etc.)

Annual membership to La 27e Région
